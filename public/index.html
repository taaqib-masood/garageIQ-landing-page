We built GarageIQ because we were tired of getting ripped off.

As expats in the UAE, we know the anxiety of a sudden car problem. You open Google Maps, see 40 pins with 4.5 stars, and cross your fingers. You don't know if they know your car's brand, you don't know if they'll overcharge you, and you don't know if that 5-star rating came from a real customer or the owner's cousins.

GarageIQ exists to kill that anxiety. We use AI not to replace human mechanics, but to organize their reputations. We structure the chaos of reviews into clear, honest signals: who to trust, what it should cost, and who specializes in your exact problem. No booking fees, no middlemen. Just the truth, structured.
